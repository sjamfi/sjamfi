VISION-MISSION STATEMENT

St. John Academy of Manila Foundation Incorporated, a private school that offers basic education was established through the efforts of people who believe that quality education is a priceless legacy that parents can hand down to their children.

THE VISION-MISSION
We the teachers and administrators of St. John Academy of Manila Foundation Inc. commit ourselves to develop holistic learners by providing them a quality education that is relevant and attuned to 21st century skills in order for them to become lifelong learners.

GOALS OBJECTIVES CORE VALUES

OUR GOALS

To develop the learners to become:

  1. family oriented
  2. responsible entrepreneurs
  3. nationalistic with the desire to preserve the culture and national heritage
  4. God fearing who put Christ as the center of his life
  5. service oriented who is compassionate and merciful
  6. environmentally sensitive with a strong advocacy to preserved life
  7. good leaders and good followers
  8. a life-long learners equipped with the 21st century skills

OUR OBJECTIVES

  1. We provide the learners with the 21st century skills in order to be relevant
  2. We provide the learners with varied and challenging activities that will develop them and globally competitive as lifelong learners
  3. We provide the learners with opportunities that will make them better decision makers

OUR CORE VALUES

  1. Sensitiveness
  2. Justness
  3. Accountability
  4. Innovativeness
  5. Fortitude
  6. Moral Integrity
