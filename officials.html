SCHOOL OFFICIALS

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

The highest policy making body of the school. It formulates and /or approves the overall school objectives and goals. It find ways and means to plan and implement all institutional programs.

THE PRESIDENT

The chief executive of the school. He is responsible in the implemntation of the policies, general plans, and guidelines formulated by the board of directors and by the Department of Education.

THE PRINCIPAL

The chief Administrative Officer and Academic Leader of the Basic Education. She is responsible for policy execution, for the articulation and implementation of the educational goals of the Basic Education, and for the work of the Basic Education under her control.

THE BASIC EDUCATION COORDINATOR /
PREFECT OF ACADEMIC AFFAIRS

Assist the Principal in matters concerning to planning, coordination, implementation and supervision of the Academic Programs of the Basic Education and supervision/evaluation of co-curricular activities. He/she supervises the work of the Grade School and the High School Coordinators. He/she assists the Principal in matters pertaining to external order, discipline, and good deportment of the students. He/she also supervises the preparation of quarterly exams and grading sheets of the faculty members.

GRADE SCHOOL / HIGH SCHOOL COORDINATOR

He/She is responsible in coordinating and monitoring the homeroom and non-academic activities of all classes in the grade school/high school level. She assist the Basic Education Prefect of Academic and Student Affairs on matters of attendance, discipline, and general deportment of students and other matters related to his/her job.

THE REGISTRAR

She is responsible for administering, supervising, and safekeeping of the academic records of the students in accordance with the regulations of the Department of Education and the school. She is alson in-charge in the registration of the students. She is directly under the supervision of the principal.

THE TREASURER

She plans, supervises and controls the financial activities of the school in accordance with the school policies and regulations. She assists the President in the preparation of the annual budget.

THE ACCOUNTANT

He takes charge of the accounting matters and assists the treasurer in the financial management of the school. He supervises the work of the cashier, bookkeeper and the accounting clerk.

THE PROPERTY CUSTODIAN

She takes charge on determining the needs of the school for supplies, materials and office equipment and keeps a record of them. She is under the supervision of the treasurer.

SECURITY OFFICERS

They supervise the school security and help ensure the safety of the students both in and out the school campus that are authorized by the school authorities. They coordinate with the school officials for activities related to earthquake drills, safekeeping of lost and found articles and other matters related to the general welfare and security.
