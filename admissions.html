Admission Procedure

In-Charge: Admission Office
The Principal gives the final approval

1

Admission Office

1. Gives parent/guardian a copy of the Bulletin Information.

2. Explains the requirements and the schedule of fees.

3. Answer questions of parent/guardian on school policies.

2

Documents and Fees

1. Parent/Guardian submits requirements and fills up the Student Application Form for Admissions.

2. Secretary evaluates requirements submitted.

3. Parent pays entrance fee of Php 200.00

4. Secretary forwards all documents to Guidance Counselor.

3

Guidance Counselor

1. Guidance Counselor checks submitted requirements

2. Guidance Counselor interviews applicant and parent.

3. Principal makes the final interview.

4. Guidance Counselor informs parent of the school's decision.

Enrollment Procedure

In-Charge: Registrar

1

Registrar

1. Parent/Guardian surrenders latest Report Card to secretary. For NEW STUDENTS, report card must be signed by principal of school last attended.


2. Admission Office Evaluates report card if student is qualified to enroll.

a. Checks if the student is qualified for grade/year level applied for.
b. Checks if all academic requirements have been completed.
c. Checks if deportment grade is passing.
d. Checks if all accounts for the previous school year have been settled.


3. Parent informs the admission office of preferred Mode of Payment


4. Registrar gives parent Registration Form and Order Slip for textbooks and School Supplies.

2

Fill-up Forms

1. Parent/Guardian fill up Order Slips for textbooks and school supplies.
2. Assisting teacher will assess completion of Registration and Order Slip Forms.

3

Submission of Forms

1. Parent/Guardian submits accomplished Registration Form and Order Slip to the Admission Office.
2. Pays needed amount for tuition fees, textbooks,  and school supplies
3. Gets payment receipt, one copy of Order Slips, and Student's Copy of Registration Form.

4

Receive Textbooks and School Supplies

Parent/Guardian presents Order Slip to assigned personnel to get textbooks and school supplies.

Schedule of Payment

• Full Payment        - full payment of the required tuition / miscellaneous fees (100%) upon enrolment


• Semestral Basis
    1st Payment        - full payment of the required (50%) tuition / miscellaneous fees upon enrolment
    2nd Payment      - balance not later than October 16 of the current school year


• Quarterly Basis
    1st Payment        - Full payment of the required downpayment of total tuition / miscellaneous fees upon enrolment

    2nd Payment     - Quarterly payment not later than August 16 of the current school year

    3rd Payment      - Quarterly payment not later than October 16 of the current school year

    4th Payment      - Quarterly payment not later than January 16 of the current school year


• Monthly Basis
    1st Payment        - Full payment of the required downpayment or as requireed upon enrolment
    2nd Payment     - Monthly payment not later than July 16 of the current school year

Issued checks which are not honoured by the bank will entail additional penalty charges as follows:

• Due to insufficient funds                - Php 500
• Due to closed account                    - Php 1,000

Check Payments are no longer accepted one week before the quarter examination. Students needing to settle their account will have to pay in cash.
